Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ReplyGPT ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects any information that you provide when using our browser extension. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By installing and using the ReplyGPT extension, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  1. Text data: When you use our extension to generate replies, the text you select is temporarily processed to generate appropriate responses.
  2. IP addresses: We collect and store your IP address when you use our service to track unique usage patterns and provide better service.
  3. Error data: Information about errors or crashes to help us fix problems with the extension.
  4. Settings: Your preferences and settings within the extension are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our extension's core functionality
  • To improve and enhance the features, performance, and user experience of our extension
  • To understand and analyze how you use our extension
  • To track unique usage patterns through IP addresses for analytical purposes
  • To develop new features, products, and services
  • To fix bugs and resolve technical issues

Data Storage and Security

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage capabilities. Your selected text is processed via our secure API and is not permanently stored on our servers.

IP addresses are collected and stored in our secure database for analytical purposes. We retain this data for up to 12 months.

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

Our extension uses AI services to generate replies. Your selected text is sent to these services for processing. These services have their own privacy policies regarding how they process and handle data.

We use analytics providers to help us understand usage patterns. These providers may receive your IP address and usage data.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you install and use our extension, you consent to the processing of your data as described in this policy.
  • Legitimate interests: We have a legitimate interest in understanding how our service is used to improve our offerings.
  • Contractual necessity: Processing is necessary to provide you with the service you have requested.

Your Choices and Rights

You have several choices regarding your information:

  • You can clear your local history through the extension interface
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time, which will remove locally stored data
  • You may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • You may have the right to restrict or object to our processing of your personal information
  • You may have the right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

Data Retention

We retain your IP address and usage data for up to 12 months. After this period, we anonymize or delete this information.

Text data processed to generate replies is not permanently stored on our servers and is deleted after processing.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the one in which you reside. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from those in your country.

We take appropriate safeguards to ensure that your personal information remains protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
